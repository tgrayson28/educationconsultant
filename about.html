Who is Dianne Cohen?

Dianne is a Virginia state certified special education k-12 teacher, who holds a master's degree in special education. As a teacher Dianne worked in both self- cotained and general education classrooms for over 17 years. She modified cirricula and adapted teaching and prompting strategies to accomodate the needs of individual learners. Dianne trained parents and school staff in implementing individualized research-based teaching and prompting methods, data collection strategies, and developmentally appropropriate measuarble goals.

Dianne worked as a special education transition specialist, working with students and families transitioning out of the high school environment. Through her career to date and personal experience, Dianne has gained extensive knowledge of educational law as well theraputic treatment for social emotional, communication, and learning needs.
